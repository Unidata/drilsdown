The explanation for high heights¶

is anticyclonic flow, especially on a large scale.

$ Z \approx `:nbsphinx-math:nabla`^{-2} \zeta_{rel} $

But absolute vorticity is more conserved, so we plot that:

$ \zeta{abs} = f+ :nbsphinx-math:`zeta`{rel} $

Simple Gaussian smoothing is used, a poor man’s version of \(\nabla^{-2}\)
